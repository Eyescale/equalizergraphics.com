We will be holding an Equalizer Birds-Of-a-Feather meeting during Eurographics'09 - mark your calendars:

Place: Eurographics 2009, TU Munich
Date: Tuesday, March 31, 15:00-16:30
Room: MI 02.13.010

Co-located with EG is the Eurographics Symposium on Parallel Graphics and Visualization, so there is yet another good reason to come to Munich!

Schedule

  • 15:00-15:20 Equalizer: Past, Present and Future, Stefan Eilemann, Eyescale Software GmbH
  • 15:20-15:40 Virtual Architecture with Equalizer and OpenSceneGraph, Julia Sigmund, University of Siegen
  • 15:40-16:00 Performance Optimizations for Image Compositing, Renato Pajarola, University of Zurich
  • 16:00-16:30 Questions and Answers, Open Discussion

Equalizer: Past, Present and Future

In this presentation we will provide an overview of the roots, current state and future work on the Equalizer parallel rendering framework. Hot new features, e.g., dynamic frame resolution and the layout API will receive special attention.

Presentation: pdf or html

Virtual Architecture with Equalizer and OpenSceneGraph

In the context of a project work for virtual architecture we combined Equalizer and OpenSceneGraph. Special consideration was given to the flexibility of the parallel rendering capabilities. The presentation reviews the synthesis of Equalizer and OpenSceneGraph for our virtual reality environment.

Performance Optimizations for Image Compositing

We will briefly review the current performance bottlenecks in Equalizer and provide some preliminary information on our research to eliminate the major issues.

Presentation: pdf or html

Q&A, Open Discussion

A relaxed get-together for current and future Equalizer developers. We encourage an open exchange of ideas, current and futures usage around Equalizer and other parallel rendering software. Getting to know each other and finding synergies between different parallel rendering applications is the other objective for this meeting.
