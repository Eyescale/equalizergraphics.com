Scalable Volume Rendering

We are pleased to announce the release of Equalizer 0.4, a framework for the development and deployment of scalable OpenGL applications. Equalizer 0.4 makes the programming of parallel OpenGL applications easier than before. Most notably, the new Programming Guide condenses all the information to write Equalizer applications. Order your hardcopy today through lulu.com!

Many additions to the programming interface facilitate the porting of existing, single-threaded OpenGL applications to Equalizer. The support for volume rendering opens the power of scalable rendering to new markets, for example medical imaging and Oil and Gas exploration. Porting of existing rendering codes has been simplified substantially by the optional per-node frame synchronization.

The full release notes for version 0.4 are available at
www.equalizergraphics.com/documents/RelNotes/RelNotes_0.4.0.html.

Commercial support, custom software development and porting services are available from Eyescale Software GmbH. Please contact info@eyescale.ch for further information.
