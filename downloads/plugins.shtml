FastJpeg Compression Plugin

Eyescale offers the FastJpeg image compression plugin for Equalizer, which enables applications to achieve interactive frame rates even for the highest display resolutions. This free version is time-limited and will stop working after 10 minutes or 10.000 frames. Please refer to the Eyescale website for further information.
