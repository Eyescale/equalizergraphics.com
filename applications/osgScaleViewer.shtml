OmniTerra
An Equalizer-based OSG application

The osgScaleViewer is a scalable, cluster-ready viewer application based on OpenSceneGraph and Equalizer. It demonstrates how to integrate OSG and Equalizer optimally to exploit the power of multi-GPU systems and visualization clusters.

Further details about osgScaleViewer can be found in this White Paper.
